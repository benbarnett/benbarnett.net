About me

I am a twenty thirty something year old software engineer based in London. This is my blog about personal stories on both client and personal projects, with which I hope to share some of the techniques and thoughts. Right now I'm working with Equal Experts helping out with engineering and consultancy.

Another is using an SVG driven Tube Map, which can be viewed over on the playground.

I find new ways of doing things fascinating and thrive from learning new skills.
